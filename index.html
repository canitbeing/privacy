Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how handles user information for the mobile application

1. No Collection of Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from our users.

The application is designed to function without requiring or storing any personally identifiable information (PII) from you. We do not have a user registration process, nor do we ask for your name, email address, phone number, device identifiers, location data, or any other information that could be used to identify you personally.

2. No Sharing or Third-Party Disclosure of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we have no personal information to share with third parties or to disclose.

3. No Data Retention or Deletion

As no personal information is collected, there is no user data for us to retain or delete.

4. No Automated Data Collection Technologies (Cookies, etc.) or Mobile Advertising Identifiers

The app does not use cookies or any other automated data collection technologies to gather personal information. Furthermore, we do not collect or utilize mobile advertising identifiers (such as ADID/IDFA) for marketing, analytics, or any other purposes.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we are committed to ensuring the general security and integrity of our application. We implement industry-standard practices to protect the app itself from unauthorized access or malicious activities.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Therefore, we comply with relevant children's online privacy protection acts worldwide, such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States, by not collecting personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page [and/or notify you through the app's announcement section]. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
