PRIVACY POLICY

Your Data Protection Matters

1. OVERVIEW

The App prioritizes user privacy protection above all else. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices clearly and completely.

2. PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTION

We do not collect any personal information whatsoever. This application does not collect:

• Personal identification data (name, email, phone)

• Usage data or analytics information

• Device information or unique identifiers

• Location information or GPS data

• Contact lists or calendar data

• Cookies or tracking technologies

3. DATA PROCESSING AND STORAGE

• All calculations and data are processed solely on your device

• No information is transmitted to external servers

• We do not use cloud storage

• Locally stored data is removed when the app is deleted

4. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

• We do not share any personal data with third parties

• Google Mobile Ads is used for advertisement delivery (see Section 9)

• Non-personally identifiable information may be processed for ad delivery

5. APP PERMISSIONS

This application uses only the following minimal permissions:

• INTERNET: For ad delivery only

• AD_ID: For ad campaign analysis only

We do not request permissions to access personal information, device storage, camera, microphone, or any private data.

6. USER RIGHTS

Since we do not collect personal information:

• Deletion requests are unnecessary

• Information modification or access requests do not apply

• Data portability rights do not apply

7. SECURITY

Because this app processes data locally without external transmission:

• There are no network security risks

• Local data follows your device's security policies

• Data remains under your complete control

• Data is encrypted in transit where applicable

8. DATA SAFETY

No data shared with third parties

• We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties

No data collected

• This application does not collect any personal, usage, or device data

Committed to follow the Play Families Policy

• Age-appropriate content throughout the app

• Compliance with Google Play Families Program requirements

Independent security review

• The app follows industry-standard security best practices

• OWASP guidelines compliance

9. ADVERTISING AND GOOGLE MOBILE ADS

This app uses Google Mobile Ads for displaying advertisements. Google may process non-personally identifiable information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google's Data Processing:

• Advertising ID (for ad campaigns)

• App interaction metrics

• Aggregate device information

For more information on Google's practices, visit their Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY PROTECTION

We do not collect personal information from users of any age, including children under 13 years old.

11. PLAY FAMILIES POLICY COMMITMENT

This application is committed to following Google Play's Families Policy:

• No collection of personal information from any users

• Age-appropriate content maintained throughout the app

• No linking to external websites without user awareness

• Compliance with all Google Play Families Program requirements

12. SECURITY REVIEW AND CERTIFICATION

This application maintains high security standards:

• Regular security best practices implementation

• Compliance with OWASP security guidelines

• Protection against common mobile application vulnerabilities

While this app has not undergone independent third-party security audits, it follows industry best practices for data protection and user safety.

13. POLICY CHANGES

When privacy policy changes occur:

• New policies will be provided through app updates

• Important changes will be communicated via in-app notifications

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. GOVERNING LAW

This policy complies with applicable privacy protection laws in your region, including GDPR, CCPA, PIPA, and other relevant regulations.

15. CONTACT US

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through the app settings or official support channels.

16. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Privacy Policy takes effect from the time of app installation and use.
