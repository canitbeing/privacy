2. PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTION

Data Collected by the App:

The App itself does not collect any personal information. This application does not collect:

• Personal identification data (name, email, phone)

• Usage data or analytics information

• Location information or GPS data

• Contact lists or calendar data

• Cookies or tracking technologies

Data Collected by Advertising Services:

This app uses Google Mobile Ads, which may collect the following data for advertising purposes:

• Advertising ID (AD_ID) - A unique device identifier for advertising

• Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, conversions)

• Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

• IP address for approximate location

This data is collected and processed by Google and their ad partners, not by the App. For details, see Section 9.

3. DATA PROCESSING AND STORAGE

App Data Processing:

• All calculations and technical data are processed solely on your device

• The app does not transmit your calculation data to external servers

• We do not use cloud storage for your data

• Locally stored preferences are removed when the app is deleted

Advertising Data Processing:

• Advertising ID and related data are transmitted to Google's ad servers

• Ad interaction data is processed by Google and their partners

• This data is stored according to Google's retention policies (see Section 9)

4. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

App Data Sharing:

• The App itself does not share any personal data with third parties

Advertising Data Sharing:

Google Mobile Ads and its ad partners may share data for advertising purposes:

• Ad performance metrics may be shared for campaign optimization

• Device information may be shared for ad targeting

You can manage your consent for data sharing through the app's consent dialog (see Section 9).

5. APP PERMISSIONS

This application uses only the following minimal permissions:

• INTERNET: For ad delivery only

• AD_ID: For ad campaign analysis only

We do not request permissions to access personal information, device storage, camera, microphone, or any private data.

9. ADVERTISING AND GOOGLE MOBILE ADS

Important: This app uses Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. While the App itself does not collect personal data, the advertising SDK collects and processes data as described below.

Data Collected by Google Mobile Ads:

• Advertising ID (AD_ID): A unique, resettable identifier for advertising purposes

• Device information: Device model, OS version, screen size, language settings

• Ad interaction data: Ad impressions, clicks, and conversions

• IP address: Used to determine approximate geographic location

• App package name and version

Purpose of Data Collection:

• Delivering relevant advertisements to users

• Measuring ad campaign performance and effectiveness

• Preventing fraud and ensuring ad quality

• Complying with legal obligations

Third-Party Ad Partners:

Google Mobile Ads may share your Advertising ID and related data with:

• Up to 141 TCF (Transparency and Consent Framework) vendors

• Up to 69 Google-certified ad partners

These partners use the data to deliver personalized advertisements and measure ad performance. Each partner operates under their own privacy policy.

Your Rights and Choices:

• Consent or refuse personalized advertising through the consent dialog

• Manage individual vendor and partner preferences

• Withdraw consent at any time through the app settings

• Reset your Advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads)

• Opt out of interest-based advertising system-wide

Data Retention:

Google and its ad partners retain advertising data according to their own retention policies, typically:

• Ad impression data: Up to 24 months

• Advertising ID associations: Until user resets the ID or opts out

Additional Resources:

• Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

• Google Ads Privacy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

• Manage Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

For a complete list of ad partners and TCF vendors, access the consent management dialog in the app.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY PROTECTION

• Personalized advertising can be disabled through consent settings

• Parents can reset the Advertising ID in device settings
