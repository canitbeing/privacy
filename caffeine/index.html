PRIVACY POLICY

Your Data Protection Matters

1. OVERVIEW

Caffeine Tracker Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Caffeine Tracker provided by xonnet studio (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer").

The App prioritizes user privacy protection. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices clearly and completely.

Key Points:

• The App does not collect personal identity information

• Caffeine records are stored locally on your device

• We use Google AdMob for advertising

• No account registration is required

2. DATA STORAGE

Local Storage:

All caffeine records are stored locally on your device using SQLite database. Data is never transmitted to external servers.

• Caffeine intake records (date, time, beverage type, amount)

• App preferences and settings

Data Control:

• All data remains under your complete control

• Data is removed when the app is uninstalled

• You can delete all data at any time through the app settings

3. AUTO BACKUP

Your data may be automatically backed up via Android Auto Backup if enabled in your device settings.

• Check Settings > Google > Backup on your device

• Backups are stored in your Google account

• Backups are encrypted and protected by Google

This feature is managed by your device's operating system, not by the app. You can disable this feature in your device settings.

4. EXPORT & IMPORT

You can manually export your data to a JSON file and import it back.

• Export: Save all caffeine records to a JSON file

• Import: Restore data from a previously exported JSON file

• Exported files are saved to your device's Download folder

This feature allows you to backup your data manually or transfer it to another device.

5. ADVERTISING & THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

This app uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

Data collected by AdMob may include:

• Advertising ID (ADID/IDFA)

• Device information and general location

• App usage data for ad performance

For more information on how Google manages data, please visit:
Google Ads Privacy Policy

Personal Inputs:
Aside from the ad-related data mentioned above, the Developer does not collect your personal caffeine intake records, name, or other sensitive personal information. Those inputs remain on your device.

6. APP PERMISSIONS

This application uses only minimal permissions:

• Storage (Android 9 and below): For JSON export/import functionality

On Android 10 and above, storage permission is not required due to Scoped Storage. We do not request permissions to access camera, microphone, contacts, or any other private data.

7. USER RIGHTS

Since we do not collect personal information:

• Deletion requests are unnecessary (data is local only)

• Information modification is done directly in the app

• Data portability is supported via JSON export

You have full control over your data at all times.

8. SECURITY

Because this app processes data locally without external transmission:

• There are no network security risks for your data

• Local data follows your device's security policies

• Data remains under your complete control

• The app follows industry-standard security best practices

9. DATA SAFETY SUMMARY

Data Collection: Minimal data collected by AdMob (Advertising ID)

Data Sharing: Data shared with Google for advertising purposes only

Data Encryption: Communications with Google services are encrypted

Data Deletion: Users can reset Advertising ID in device settings

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13 years old.

Since no data is collected or transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.
